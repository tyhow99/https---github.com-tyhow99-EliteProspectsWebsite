About EP Player Tracker

The player tracker is a powerful tool that provides daily updates on your favorite players. Each game a player competes in is included with information about personal stats in the game, as well as opponent and score. A player game log is added roughly 1-2 hours after a game has ended. Default view shows your bookmarked players.
